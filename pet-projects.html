The alpaca brief.

This is the less corporate corner of the site. Smaller budgets. Bigger personalities. Still strategy. Just with more hay.

Family Alpaca Farm · Paid Media Initiative

Local marketing, but make it fluffy.

I ran a paid media initiative for my family's alpaca farm, translating a very real, very charming offline experience into a digital campaign people could actually discover.

The brief was simple: help more people find the farm. The fun part was figuring out how to package the magic — the animals, the setting, the family story, and the surprise of realizing there are alpacas nearby — into something that felt warm, local, and worth leaving the house for.

It was a reminder that the same strategy muscles work everywhere. You still need audience insight, a clear message, a reason to care, and a little bit of taste. The only difference is that sometimes the client has four legs and an attitude.
