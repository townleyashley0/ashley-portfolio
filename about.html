About

The person behind the systems.

I am happiest when I am learning how something works — a business system, a campaign, a recipe, a city, a family story, or whatever strange rabbit hole I have decided to follow that week.

I come from a family that taught me to be curious, capable, and a little bit scrappy. My mom runs an alpaca farm, so I have a soft spot for animals, open space, and the kind of practical chaos that makes you better at solving problems in real life.

At home, I am usually with my boys, Mr. Cottonball and Biscuit. Mr. Cottonball likes walks and outdoor supervision. Biscuit is more of a private-suite-with-no-doors visionary.

Other things about me: I love Pilates, vintage fashion, cooking pasta, building overly specific AI experiments, and collecting vintage vinyls. My favorite find is Sgt. Pepper’s Lonely Hearts Club Band by The Beatles.
