About

A decade of building what the work needed next.

I work at the intersection of strategy, operations, and media — translating ambiguity into systems, campaigns, and decisions that make teams sharper.

My background spans global brands, independent consulting, regulated industries, performance marketing, and AI-enabled operations. I am drawn to moments when the old way of working stops being enough: mobile changing consumer behavior, content changing brand expectations, performance data changing investment decisions, and AI changing how teams operate.

Outside the work, I am usually finding vintage fashion, walking Mr. Cottonball, perfecting pasta, going to Pilates, or building overly specific AI experiments because I got curious.
