05 — About

Strategist. Operator. Builder. Human person.

Professionally, I sit somewhere between strategy and operations. I like making sense of messy things: the campaign, the budget, the client problem, the stakeholder tension, the process that is quietly making everyone insane.

Personally, I am much less buttoned-up. I have two cats, Mr. Cottonball and Biscuit, who operate as tiny household executives. I love Pilates, yoga, thrifting, cooking, baking, gardening, fashion, beauty, and traveling whenever I can make the calendar behave.

I care about taste and systems in equal measure. A good campaign, a good outfit, a good dinner, a good workflow — to me, they all come from paying attention.
