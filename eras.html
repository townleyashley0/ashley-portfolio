The through line.

The work looks different depending on the chapter. The pattern is the same: step into the change, understand what is shifting, and build what helps the work move.

01

The Smartphone Era

Samsung, London 2012, Galaxy devices, fashion partnerships, and the years when the phone became the center of modern life.

02

The Content Era

Canon, BuzzFeed, Project Imagination, and the moment brands stopped only advertising and started publishing, partnering, and entertaining.

03

The Regulated Era

Novartis and pharmaceutical media, where the work had to be precise, compliant, fast-moving, and carefully aligned.

04

The Independent Era

Building a consulting practice from the ground up, owning the client relationship, the strategy, the execution, and the outcome.

05

The AI Operations Era

Forecasting, budget management, campaign monitoring, and workflow systems built for the next version of how marketing teams operate.
